Movement

  • w/k - Move up.
  • a/h - Move left.
  • s/j - Move down.
  • d/l - Move right.
  • >   - Climb down stairs.
  • <   - Climb up stairs.

Magic

  • i - Cast an incantation.

Press esc to dismiss

  • Range = (experience + 1) * 2
  • Experience: Gain 2 experience per kill.

alohomora - Open all locked doors in range.

  • Range = 1
  • Experience: Gain 1 experience per door opened.
